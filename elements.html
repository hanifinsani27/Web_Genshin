Elements

Anemo

Anemo has Elemental Reactions with the elements Electro, Pyro, Hydro and Cryo. Depending on the particular talent, it can Swirl or absorb the element or do both.
Swirl: Deals Elemental DMG of the involved element while spreading the involved elemental status to nearby enemies. Many Anemo characters and enemies also have the following skills:
Elemental Absorption: Absorbs an element and becomes infused with that element, dealing damage of that type. The absorbed element can trigger further reactions, including Swirl. Displacement (Crowd Control): The ability to displace mobs of entities by knocking them back or pulling them in. Many Anemo users especially have the ability to create Vacuum Fields that pull in enemies within a radius.

Cryo

Having two Cryo characters in the group results in the Elemental Resonance:
Shattering Ice: Affected by Electro for 40% less time. Increases CRIT Rate against enemies that are Frozen or affected by Cryo by 15%

Dendro

Dendro has Elemental Reactions with the element Pyro, which is Burning.
Pyro Burning: Deals Pyro DMG over time. The grass is considered a Dendro object and will spread the burn effect around the area.

Electro

Having two Electro characters in the group results in the Elemental Resonance:
High Voltage: Affected by Hydro for 40% less time. Superconduct, Overloaded, and Electro-Charged have a 100% chance to generate an Electro Elemental Particle (CD: 5s).

Geo

Having two Geo characters in the group results in the Elemental Resonance:
Enduring Rock: Increases shield strength by 15%. Additionally, characters protected by a shield will have the following special characteristics: DMG dealt increased by 15%, dealing DMG to enemies will decrease their Geo RES by 20% for 15s.

Hydro

Having two Hydro characters in the group results in the Elemental Resonance:
Soothing Water: Affected by Pyro for 40% less time. Increases incoming healing by 30%.

Pyro

Having two Pyro characters in the group results in the Elemental Resonance:
Fervent Flames: Affected by Cryo for 40% less time. Increases ATK by 25%.
